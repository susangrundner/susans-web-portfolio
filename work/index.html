HTML/CSS


Jubilee Austen – Jubilee is the first website I built. This is strictly a fictional site and fictional person, but a great example of my use of HTML5 and CSS. I love the cool, crisp, cleaness of this website.




Rogue Pickings – This is a fictional site, but another good example of HTML5, CSS, and this time, it was built from a comp. The images were extracted from the comp via Photoshop and loaded back into the site image folder. The links don't work, and for this project, they weren't meant to, but I can very easily add pages and activate those links. I love how the color green was used. Not too overbearing, but just enough to smell the green beans.



RESPONSIVE DESIGN


Aisha's Web Page – This was a Skillcrush exercise in Flexbox layouts. Just click out of full-page mode and start minimizing the page. Then behold the magic of Flexbox!



Rogue Responsive – I took the Rogue Pickings website and made it responsive. Like the Aisha site, this site can adapt to any size mobile device, as well as desktops.
